Campaign Recap

A chronicle order of the campaign!

Session 0: The Beginning

The journey began around the table — introductions, laughter, and forging new heroes. Players created their characters, shared backstories, and stepped into the world of Faerûn for the very first time.

Session 1: Wake the Mind

The party awoke aboard a crashing nautiloid, each marked by a parasite and haunted by fragmented memories. Together, they fought their way through mind flayers and demons, and made a desperate escape before the ship shattered over Faerûn.
